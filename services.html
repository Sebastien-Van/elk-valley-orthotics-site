Services

Treatment built around your feet, not a catalogue

Every visit starts with an assessment. From there, treatment is tailored to your footwear, terrain, and goals — whether that's a ski season or a shift on your feet all day.

Custom Orthotics

Scanned, molded, and milled in-house for your exact gait and footwear.

Learn more

Two-visit process: assessment and casting, then fitting and adjustment. Compatible with ski boots, hiking boots, work boots, and everyday shoes. Most extended health plans cover a portion of the cost.

Gait & Pressure Assessment

Pressure-plate analysis plus a hands-on physical exam.

Learn more

We map how weight moves through your foot at each stride, identify compensation patterns, and trace pain back to its source — often the knee or hip, not just the foot itself.

Ski & Boot Fittings

Getting your orthotic to actually work inside winter footwear.

Learn more

We coordinate directly with local ski and outdoor retailers so your custom orthotic fits your ski boots, not just a slip-on shoe on the exam table.

Diabetic Foot Care

Protective footwear and orthotics for at-risk feet.

Learn more

Pressure-relieving orthotics and footwear assessments designed to reduce ulcer risk, with regular check-ins built into your care plan.

Pediatric Assessments

Gait checks and orthotics for growing feet.

Learn more

Early gait evaluation for kids showing in-toeing, out-toeing, or recurring complaints of leg and foot pain during growth spurts.

Follow-Up & Adjustments

Included check-ins to fine-tune your orthotic over time.

Learn more

Feet change with activity level and season. Adjustment appointments are quick, usually same-day, and included for the life of your orthotic.
