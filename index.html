What We Treat

Care built around how you actually move

Custom Orthotics

3D-scanned and hand-finished for your gait, footwear, and terrain.

Learn more

We start with a full gait and pressure-mapping assessment, then mold and mill your orthotics locally — no shipping devices out of the valley and waiting weeks. Most patients are fitted within two visits.

Gait & Movement Assessment

In-depth analysis of how you stand, walk, and load your feet.

Learn more

Using pressure-plate technology and a hands-on physical exam, we identify the root cause of pain — whether it starts at the foot, knee, or hip — before recommending treatment.

Sport & Recreation Fittings

Ski boots, hiking boots, and work boots fitted to your orthotic.

Learn more

We work directly with local ski and outdoor shops so your orthotic actually fits inside the footwear you rely on all winter, not just a running shoe on the exam table.
