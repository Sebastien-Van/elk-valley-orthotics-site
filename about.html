About Us

Foot care that knows the terrain

Elk Valley Orthotics was built for a community that spends its life on its feet — on trails, on skis, and on the job. Here's how we work.

Our Story

Founded on the valley floor

Elk Valley Orthotics opened to fill a gap the closest cities couldn't: foot care built around a mountain lifestyle. Skiers, hikers, and shift workers all load their feet differently than the general population — and off-the-shelf solutions rarely hold up to that.

Every orthotic we make is scanned, molded, and finished locally, so you're not waiting weeks for a lab three provinces away.

Our Approach

Assess first, prescribe second

We never fit an orthotic without a full gait and pressure assessment first. That means understanding your footwear, your terrain, and what's actually causing discomfort — not just treating the symptom.

Follow-up adjustments are included with every custom orthotic, because feet (and trails) change with the seasons.
